Support & crisis resources

Diaspora One Nation is not an emergency service. If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services or a crisis hotline in your country.

  • Talk to a doctor, therapist, or counselor where you live.
  • Reach out to trusted family, friends, or community leaders.
  • Use national hotlines for suicide prevention or addiction support.
